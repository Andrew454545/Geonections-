Groups: 4 Mistakes: 4

🌍 Welcome to Geonections!

Make four groups of four — each group is from the same country.

How to play

  1. Click a tile to select it (it gets a color tag). Click again to deselect.
  2. Select in sets of four.
  3. Correct sets lock in. Wrong sets use 1 mistake. You have 4 mistakes total.

Helpful controls

  • Double-click a tile or press F to toggle full-screen view on the last tile you’ve selected.
  • Shuffle reorders unsolved tiles.
  • Share appears after the game ends.

Warning: This game is intended for geoguessr players, and is intended to be at least mildly difficult for the best among them, so don’t feel bad if you can’t get one.

Good luck!
